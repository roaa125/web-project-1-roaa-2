What is Islam about?

Islam is not just a religion! Islam is a major global religion! Islam means "surrender" in Arabic, highlighting the faith's core idea that the believers (Muslims) should submit to Allah's (God's) will. Muslims believe Allah is the sole God, the world's creator, sustainer, and restorer.
